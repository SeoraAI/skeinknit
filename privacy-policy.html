Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 13, 2026

Skein ("we," "our," or "the app") is a knitting toolkit developed by Nice People Collective LLC. This policy describes how we handle your information.

What We Collect

Skein stores your knitting data — projects, patterns, yarn stash entries, gauge measurements, and grading calculations — locally on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or store your personal data on external servers.

Subscriptions

Skein offers optional in-app subscriptions (Designer Pro) processed entirely through Apple's App Store. We do not collect or have access to your payment information. Subscription management and billing are handled by Apple.

Third-Party Services

Skein may use Apple's StoreKit framework for in-app purchases and RevenueCat for subscription analytics. These services may collect anonymized usage data in accordance with their own privacy policies. We do not sell, share, or transfer your personal information to any third party for advertising or marketing purposes.

Analytics

We may collect anonymized, aggregated usage data (such as which features are used most frequently) to improve the app. This data cannot be used to identify you personally.

Data Storage

All user-created content (projects, stash, patterns, calculations) is stored locally on your device. If you delete the app, this data is permanently removed. We do not maintain backups of your data on our servers.

Children's Privacy

Skein is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Your Rights

Since your data is stored locally on your device, you have full control over it at all times. You can delete any data within the app or remove all data by deleting the app.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date.

Contact

Questions about this policy? Contact us at support@skeinknit.com.
